Timeline of your training year

What happens exactly and when will depend on your specific training provider. But you can expect the following to happen as part of your teacher training, regardless of your course.

SCITT timeline

  1. July to Aug

    Induction during summer

    A lot of SCITTs will start the process of your induction before and during the summer holidays.

  2. July to Aug

    Receive plan of the year

    Your SCITT may also give you a detailed timetable that outlines each week in your training year and what to expect.

  3. Sept

    Start first placement school

    When you first start your placement, you'll mainly be observing. As you grow more confident, you'll start to take on more responsibilites.

    View details of the event on 15 May 2024 at 4:01pm

  4. Oct

    Formative assessments

    You may submit a formative assessment at this point in preparation for the real one in December.

  5. Dec

    End of first placement

    You’ll finish your first placement and submit your summative QTS assessment (and PGCE assessment if you’re doing one).

  6. Jan

    Start 2nd placement

    You’ll begin your second placement at a different school and focus on different aspects of teaching.

    Some trainees begin applying for their first role.

  7. Feb

    Formative assessments

    You may submit a formative assessment at this point in preparation for the real one in April.

  8. April

    Summative assessments

    You’ll submit your second summative assessment.

    Some trainees may return to their first school while others will stay in their second placement school till the end of May.

  9. May

    End of placements

    You’ll finish your placement at the end of May and spend the next month preparing for your final assessments in June.

  10. June

    Final assessments

    After completing your final assessments you’ll hopefully gain QTS and begin looking for your new role as an ECT.
